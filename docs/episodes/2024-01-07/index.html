Sparks of Dawn

2024-01-07

Morning spilled over the silent suburb with a hush of orange light. Lara stepped outside, coffee in hand, and found the sky scribbled with contrails. She imagined each line as a letter from a distant author, arriving in streaks of vapor. The air held a promise she could not name. Neighbors stirred, shutters creaked, and the sleepy street learned the shape of day. She smiled at the small drama and decided to write it down before the page of sky turned blank.
