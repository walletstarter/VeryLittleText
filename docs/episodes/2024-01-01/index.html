Snow Instructions

2024-01-01

Fresh snow stacked along the sidewalk like pages in a manual. Milo followed the untouched path and pretended each print he left was a step in a recipe. The morning was quiet, except for the whispers of flakes settling on his coat. At the corner he paused, reading the street as if it held instructions for living slowly. A passerby nodded, both of them apprentices to winter's brief lesson. By the time he reached home, the manual had vanished under the noon sun, but the steps remained memorized.
