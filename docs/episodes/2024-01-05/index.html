Streetlight Witness

2024-01-05

The broken streetlight on Pine Avenue clicked each evening, deciding whether to glow. Aaron walked his dog under its indecision, waiting for the instant of illumination. The lamp knew the neighborhood's secrets: who came home late, who cried in parked cars, who practiced dance moves in the empty lot. Tonight it flickered to life as a cyclist crashed near the curb. The pool of light held them both, revealing scraped knees and a bouquet of spilled groceries. The lamp hummed, a silent witness, until the scene resolved and darkness returned.
